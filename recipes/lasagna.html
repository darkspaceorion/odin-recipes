Spicey Lasagna

Tasty Homemade recipe

This is a really great and spicy recipe from my ancestor! Enjoy.

Ingerdients

  • Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Pasta
  • Red Sauce
  • whiskey

Now follow the directions below to make the good stuff

Instructions

  1. Open whiskey bottle and pour a glass
  2. Boil water for pasta, then cook pasta
  3. Brown ground beef
  4. Mix all Ingerdients in pan
  5. Cook in oven 20 min at 350 degrees
